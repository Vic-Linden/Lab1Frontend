About me

The student

A future developer who believes in the importance of discipline and structure. Currently, I am pouring all my energy into my Fullstack .NET studies, with a deep interest in understanding the underlying logic and architecture of software.
I don’t just stay within the classroom, I actively seek out more advanced knowledge by participating in Hackathons and connecting with other developers in the community to explore the frontiers of AI and automation. For me, programming is about continuous growth and solving complex problems.

The Balance

To maintain this level of intensity, I believe that balance is essential. When I’m not deep coding or exploring, I recharge by being active and creative in other ways. Whether it's the endurance of running, the passion and loyalty in football, the happiness of baking and cooking or simply reading my favorite fantasy book, these activities gives me the motivation to return to my projects with a sharp mind.
